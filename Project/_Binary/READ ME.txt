DIRECTIONS:

-Tab cycles game states - START, IN-PLAY, END ROUND, END GAME
-IN-PLAY:
	-Game will reset if previously played
	-Arrow keys (left and right) move the puck
	-Pressing space will go from movement to rotation
	-Arrow keys after, change rotation
	-Pressing space a second time brings up power meter, which determines strength of shot
	-Pressing space a third time shoots the puck
	-Press space a fourth time to bring up other player's ball
	-Both players get 3 shots each
-END ROUND and END GAME are essentially the end states after both players finish shooting
